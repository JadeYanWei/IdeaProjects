Track order

When you place an order with Ekseption.com, we will send you an email confirming
receipt of your order and containing the details of your order, such as the price, delivery costs, form of payment, delivery
address and tracking number provided by DHL.

Reterns

If you are unhappy with your purchase in any way. No problem! You will have 14 days
to request a refund, counting from the date of delivery. You will have to send us an e-mail to return@ekseption.es specifying
your order number and we will inform you all at the time.
We will take care of all the return management and we will refund the total minus the original shipping and return costs. The return
cost is the same price as the shipping. All returns will be only
accepted once we have previously managed them with our courriers.Some items marked down are considered
Final Sale and cannot be returned as outlined in the product details. Vintage items
of 150$ or less cannot be returned. First size exchange without shipping
costs.We can only make a refund or exchange non-faulty items
that are in their original condition, have not
been worn, altered or washed, with all tags attached. The merchandise must be returned in the original package.

Taxes and duties

In orders to United States & Canada, prices are VAT excluded. Taxes and duties are
calculated depending on the shipping address. Ekseption is not responsabile for this payment. The return shipping fee is not
covered by us. Orders over $800 to United States are subjected to customs and taxes.
